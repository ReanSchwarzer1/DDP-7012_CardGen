>> PHASE 0: GAME START (AUCTION)

ENERGY BIDDING

Each player starts with 30 energy (represented in the player boards). Players blind bid Energy to determine turn order (cannot exceed 5 bid energy by each player).

  • Highest bid = chooses their position first (1st, 2nd, 3rd, or 4th)
  • Ties = simultaneous reveal of a d6 roll (highest wins)
Compensation:
  • Position 1: No bonus
  • Position 2: +1 resource for any resource they choose + 1 energy
  • Position 3: +2 Energy + 2 resource for any resource they choose
  • Position 4: +2 Energy + 1 Scavenge Card + 2 resource for any resource they choose

Action Selection: All players, based on the turn order, roll two dice (one die the first time to decide between sectors A-F) and two dice together to decide the room number. (e.g., C + 8 = C8).

>> OPERATIONAL CYCLE (PHASES 1-6)

01 BRIEFING (PLANNING)

Location Check: All players must announce their current room or tile location.
Action Selection: All players (Human and Possessed) must also announce their intended action.
Note: Once placed, actions are locked. No take-backs.

02 PLAYER ACTIONS

HUMAN PLAYERS:
  • Move: Move 2 tiles (Free). Additional tiles cost 1 Energy each.
  • Search Room: Draw 1 Scavenge Card. Costs 2 Energy. (Triggers Noise).
  • Open Door: Costs 1-2 Energy.
  • Hide: Become Invisible. Locked in room until Alien passes. (Next turn: No action).
  • Yell: Force Alien toward your specific tile. Costs 2 Energy.
  • Role Ability: Use a specific Role Ability.
  • Use Item: Deploy a Scavenge item.
POSSESSED PLAYERS:
Use Command Console (Sabotage Deck).
Twisted Role Action: Corrupted version of original role.
Sensory Overload: Force Hidden player in room/adjacent/2 tiles to reveal.
Direct Interference: "Destroy Sanity" roll if in same room.

03 THE THREAT

Tier Check:
  • Tier 1 (Turns 1–3): Standard + Tier 1 Hunt + Hazard.
  • Tier 2 (Turns 4–6): Tier 2 Hunt + Hazard + Evolve. (Remove Tier 1 Hunt).
  • Tier 3 (Turn 7+): Tier 3 Hunt added.
Draw Logic: 1 Base + 1 per Possessed + 1 if Alarm Active + 1 per Clone.
Late Game: +1 card for every 2 turns after Turn 7.

04 ALIEN RESOLUTION

The Alien executes the instructions from Phase 3.
Action: Alien moves and acts according to the resolved cards (toward Noise or specific Rooms).

05 ENCOUNTERS

Step A: The Encounter Roll (Escape): Player flees to an adjacent tile and is Auto-Hidden for 1 turn. (Always happens in the first capture).

Step B: Capture Consequences:
  • First Capture: Player released, Alarm Triggers. All players Visible (No Hiding 2 turns). Energy Drain +1. Alien Deck +1 card permanently.
  • Second Capture (Possession): Player becomes Possessed. Receives Sabotage Deck. Alien Deck +1 card draw per turn. Alarm triggers.

06 PLAYER RESPONSE

Triggers if Alien within 3 tiles (or Possessed within 2 tiles).
  • Yell (-1 Energy): Distract Alien to move towards player.
  • Guard: Adjacent player rolls d6 (3-6 Success) to take Capture event.
  • Flash Retreat (-8 Energy): Move 2 tiles ANY direction (Must have 1E remaining).
  • Desperate Strike (-2 Energy + 1 Item): Roll d6 (4-6 Push Back, 1-3 Attack, 1 Auto-Possession).
  • Barricade (-3 Energy + 3 Resource): Seal room entrance for 2 turns.

>> WIN CONDITIONS
  • If 2+ players are not possessed and reach the escape pods by turn 12 or earlier, they win.
  • If only 1 player reaches the escape pod (not possessed), it is a win condition only for the possessed players.
  • If more than 2 players reach the escape pods by turn 12, they escape the space station before it crashes into the nearest star.
  • If the number of possessed players is 3 or more, the possessed players win.
  • If all players turn possessed (especially before turn 12), the possessed players win. Turn counter automatically counts to turn 12, and the alien absorbs them.
