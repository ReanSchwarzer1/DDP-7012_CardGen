>> OPERATIONAL CYCLE (6 PHASES)

01 BRIEFING

1. Location Check: Announce your current Room/Tile.
2. Action Selection: Place Action Card Face-Down.
WARNING: Decisions are locked. No changes allowed.

02 EXECUTION

HUMAN: Resolve Chosen Action.
  • Move (1 Tile Free, +1 Energy per extra)
  • Search (2 Energy -> Draw Item/Resource)
  • Door (1-2 Energy -> Creates Noise)
  • Hide (Invisible until move/reveal)
  • Role Ability / Item
POSSESSED: Twisted Action, Sensory Overload (Reveal hidden), or Interference (Sanity Roll).

03 THE THREAT

GM/System Draws Cards:
Count = 1 Base + 1 per Possessed + Alarm Status.
Resolves: Hunt Cards, Swarm Instincts (Chain Draw), and Environmental Hazards.
UPLINK: Possessed players may play Interference cards now.

04 RESOLUTION

Alien Movement:
The Entity moves X tiles according to the Hunt Cards drawn in Phase 3.

Prioritizes: Noise > Sight > Energy > Random

05 ENCOUNTER

Triggers if Alien enters Player Tile.

1st Encounter: ESCAPE. Player flees 1 tile (Hidden). ALARM TRIGGERS (+1 Threat Level).

2nd Encounter: POSSESSION. Player joins the Hive Mind.

Biologist Exorcism available here if conditions met.

06 REACTION

Triggers if Alien is < 3 Tiles away (and player is not in Safe Room).

Available Instant Actions:
  • Yell: Distract Alien to your tile.
  • Trap: Activate set trap (Stun).
  • Guard: Ally takes the hit (Roll d6: 3+ Success).
